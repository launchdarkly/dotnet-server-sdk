public enum EvaluationReasonKind
Public Enumeration EvaluationReasonKind
Members
  Member nameValueDescription
OFF0 Indicates that the flag was off and therefore returned its configured off value.
FALLTHROUGH1 Indicates that the flag was on but the user did not match any targets or rules.
TARGET_MATCH2 Indicates that the user key was specifically targeted for this flag.
RULE_MATCH3 Indicates that the user matched one of the flag's rules.
PREREQUISITE_FAILED4 Indicates that the flag was considered off because it had at least one prerequisite flag that either was off or did not return the desired variation.
ERROR5 Indicates that the flag could not be evaluated, e.g. because it does not exist or due to an unexpected error. In this case the result value will be the default value that the caller passed to the client.
