Remarks
This is currently just a wrapper for using the Newtonsoft.Json parsing method and converting the resulting value to LdValue. However, it may not always be implemented in that way. It rethrows the underlying parser's JsonException as an ArgumentException to avoid surfacing dependencies on that third-party API.
